Do you provide non-IT training courses?

Yes, we provide comprehensive non-IT training courses covering a range of positions such as Recruiters, Delivery Managers, Account Managers, Business Development Executives, Business Development Managers, and Client Acquisition Managers.

What are the prerequisites for enrolling in your training courses?

The prerequisites for our training courses vary depending on the course. Some courses require prior knowledge in specific programming languages or tools, while others do not require any prior experience. Please check the course description for more information on prerequisites.

Is the training course fee refundable?

Our refund policy depends on the course. Please refer to our terms and conditions for detailed information.

How can I enroll in a training course?

You can enroll in a training course by visiting our website and filling out the enrollment form.

What is the duration of your training courses?

The duration of our training courses varies. Please check the specific course details for more information.
